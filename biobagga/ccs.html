The Closed Compost System Process - Technological Advantages

Controlled Environment

  • CCS is a closed "container"
  • Not influenced by the weather, rain and wind (dispersal)
  • Controlled run off
  • Controlled odour emission

Controlled Process

  • Temperature control
  • Forced oxygen supply

Space-efficient

  • Simple location is sufficient (firm yarding/water/electricity)

Simple Technology

  • Proven "bagging" technology
  • Economic Advantages

Low Investment, Cost Effective Result

  • This system works! Proven in multiple countries, locations and climates.
  • Uncomplicated. Mix sludge and carbon source - simple. Bag it, aerate it - and wait.
  • No greenwaste issues - many other carbon sources available in NZ - wood works better in most cases.
  • 25 - 30 cum (20%DS) sludge per (CM1.5) bag - far more cost effective than most landfill fees.

Short Processing Time

  • 12 to 15 weeks between start of process and end product

Products with Composting Difficulties

  • Products with odour problems can be easily processed

Flexible System

  • Portable if required
